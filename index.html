Nightly Divine Ceremony

Ganga Aarti

Every evening as the sun dissolves into the Ganga, Dashashwamedh Ghat transforms into something beyond words. Seven priests in perfect synchrony — fire meets river, chants rise above the water — and for a few moments the whole world falls still.

It is not a performance. It is a prayer. And once witnessed, it lives in you forever.

Getting to Kashi

How to Reach Varanasi

By Air

Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (VNS), 26 km. Direct flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bengaluru & Chennai daily.

By Train

Varanasi Junction (BSB) connected nationwide. Kashi Express, Shiv Ganga Express and Vande Bharat from major cities.

By Road

NH-19 & NH-29 connect Varanasi. Volvo buses from Lucknow (286 km), Prayagraj (120 km) and Patna (230 km).

By River

Seasonal services from Ramnagar. Private country boats and tourist launches from all major ghats.
