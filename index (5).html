Sacred Ghats of Varanasi

Varanasi's 84 ghats line the western bank of the Ganges. Here are the most revered.

01 · Main Ghat
Dashashwamedh
The principal ghat, famed for the evening Ganga Aarti ceremony
02 · Cremation
Manikarnika
Sacred cremation ghat — fires have burned here for thousands of years
03 · Southern End
Assi Ghat
Morning Subah-e-Banaras aarti; confluence of Assi and Ganga
04 · Ancient
Harishchandra
Named after the truthful king; one of two cremation ghats
05 · Submerged
Scindia Ghat
Famous for its partially submerged Shiva temple — a unique sight
06 · Five Rivers
Panchganga
Confluence of five sacred rivers; Alamgir Mosque rises above
07 · Historic
Man Mandir
Raja Man Singh's 1600 CE palace with an astronomical observatory
08 · South Indian
Kedar Ghat
Favourite of South Indian pilgrims; Kedara temple nearby
09 · Modern
Namo Ghat
New development with yoga deck, amphitheatre and gardens
10 · Pilgrimage
Raj Ghat
Northern end ghat; quiet and spiritual in the early morning
Dashashwamedh Ghat · Varanasi
Nightly Ceremony

The Ganga Aarti

Every evening at Dashashwamedh Ghat, seven priests simultaneously perform the Ganga Aarti — a breathtaking ritual of fire, flowers, conch shells and Vedic chants offered to the sacred River Ganges.

Watching from a boat as the lamps reflect on the dark river is an experience unlike anything else in the world. The morning Subah-e-Banaras aarti at Assi Ghat offers a quieter, equally divine alternative.

Getting There

How to Reach Varanasi

By Air

Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (VNS), Babatpur — 26 km from city. Direct flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bengaluru and Chennai daily.

By Train

Varanasi Junction (BSB) is well-connected nationwide. Kashi Express, Shiv Ganga Express and Vande Bharat from Delhi, Mumbai and Kolkata.

By Road

NH-19 and NH-29 connect Varanasi. UPSRTC Volvo buses from Lucknow (286 km), Prayagraj (120 km) and Patna (230 km).

By River

Seasonal boat services from Ramnagar. Private boats and tourist launches are available from all major ghats along the riverfront.
